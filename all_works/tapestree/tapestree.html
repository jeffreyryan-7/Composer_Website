Jeffrey Ryan

Tapestree

Multi-percussion solo | ~7' | 2023

Tapestree features a setup of only unpitched, wooden instruments: 4 wood slats, 5 woodblocks, 4 log drum tongues, and 1 very low wood played with a foot pedal. The solo is inspired by a blend of Xenakis' percussion writing and songs I learned at the Pháp Lâm Temple in Rockford Illinois.

This piece is currently unpublished. Please email jcr101402@gmail.com with any questions.
