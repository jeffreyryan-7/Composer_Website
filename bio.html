Jeffrey Ryan

Bio

I was born near Chicago and grew up in Franklin Tennessee. In middle school, I started playing music for the first time and developed a deep love for percussion and improvisation. By the end of high school, I had written and premiered a marimba solo at an arts showcase and a percussion duet on a percussion ensemble concert.

I currently study percussion at Northwestern under She-e Wu. During my time there, I've written and premiered five solos and three chamber works, with help from members of the percussion studio.

Right now, I'm working on branching out to write for other instruments and exploring composition with emphasis on performer presence (see "Essays: Becoming Fertile Rain").

If you have any questions about past compositions or interest/requests for future works, don't hesitate to reach out!
